== Description ==

The only shipping app you will ever need for your business. You can “Shiip” with top logistics companies at half their standard rates


== Installation ==

1. Upload `shiipwordpress` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Goto woocommerce setings section and select shipping
4. Select `SHIIP` and enter your goshiip.com login details 
5. Also enter your address under the origin location and phone number

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= What is SHIIP? =

SHiiP connects individuals and small businesses with the best delivery options, discounted shipping, and real-time rate comparison from all leading carriers, plus on-demand tracking, customer shipping notifications, seamless handling of returns, and more. 

= Is shiip available in other countries? =
Shiip is currently available in Nigeria and US. 

= What should I do if i forget my password? =
We know that sometimes you can forget your password, either for a shortwhile or permanently so we have made it easy for you to reset your password while ensuring that your account continues to remain secure. In the event that you forgot your password. Visit https://app.goshiip.com/forgot-password enter your email and click on the reset password button. Check your email for an instruction to create a new password and access your account.
